DIRECTORATE OF TECHNICAL EDUCATION

The directorate was established at Kanpur with the aim to uplift the standard of technical education in Uttar Pradesh. Development of technical education in a planned way with a well-coordinated manner, according to the local industrial needs was another objective of establishing this directorate. Directorate works as nodal agency to provide resources for utilization of increasing interference of computerization and use of modern sophisticated technologies. Thus the aim of directorate speaks more than the expansion of resources and points towards rising of quality. Directorate works towards increasing involvement of women in technical education, and to ensure availability of technical manpower for the small scale industries of the sector so that equilibrium between technical resources and output can be formed.
