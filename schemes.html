SCHEMES OF GOVERNMENT OF INDIA

During financial year 2008-09, under the GOI scheme “Submission on Polytechnics action of skill development", total 41 polytechnics have been approved for construction and equipped with establishment related services. Out of these 41, 38 polytechnics are functioning now.

During financial year 2010-11, 52 Women’s Hostels were approved under the GOI scheme'Construction of Women's Hostel in Existing Polytechnics' and out of which construction work of 40 Women’s Hostel has been completed and these hostels have been made public useable.

Under the GOI scheme "Construction of Women's Hostel in Existing Polytechnics", DPR for reinforcement of 45 polytechnics (old institutes) were approved and action is being taken to reinforce them.

GOI scheme “Community Development Through Polytechnics” is approved for total 59 (42 Government and 17 aided) polytechnics. Under this scheme short term (06 months) technical training is imparted to youths (boys/girls) in order to make them able to earn their livelihood by doing any technical work. Under this training, generally short term and employment oriented programs are conducted like Motor Winding, Wiring, Auto Mechanic, Computer Typing, Beautician, Carpentry and Plumbing.

UPGRADATION / STRENGTHENING OF OLD AND NEW INSTITUTES

Under the GOI scheme “Establishing the new Polytechnics” for constructed/under construction new polytechnics, a grant of Rs. 117.72 Crore is received since 2012-13 to 2014-15 for 39 Polytechnics against 41 institutes for supply of equipment, interior, books, furniture etc. Amount of Rs. 59.69 Crore has been expended against the received amount till 31.03.2019. Remaining amount is being provisioned through budget. Proposals for administrative approval of Rs. 7.45 Crore against amount of Rs. 8.00 Crore as proposed in the budget for FY 2019-20, have been submitted to government and proposal for administrative approval for expense of Rs. 0.53 Crore will be sent to government shortly.

Under the GOI scheme “Upgradation/Reinforcement of Old Institutes” a grant of Rs. 45.00 Crore has been received for 49 institutes against 56 selected old institutes since FY 2012-13 to FY 2014-15. At present process is on for upgradation/reinforcement of 45 institutes. Rs. 22.84 Crores have been expended against received amount 45.00 Crore, till 31.03.2019. Remaining amount is being provisioned through budget. Proposals for administrative approval of Rs. 2.15 Crore against amount of Rs. 5.00 Crore as proposed in the budget for FY 2019-20, have been submitted to government and proposal for administrative approval for expense of Rs. 1.57 Crore will be sent to government shortly.

SCHEMES OF STATE GOVERNMENT

For growth of technical education 28 Government polytechnics have been approved by state government and under the S.C.S.P./M.M.I.T. scheme, 19 polytechnics have been approved by the financial cooperation of Social Welfare Department, out of which 35 polytechnics are now functioning.

With the objective of providing hostel facilities to students, 15 Male hostels were approved under S.C.S.P./M.M.I.T. scheme. This job is being done through 100% financial cooperation of Social welfare Department and 10 hostels have been made functional.

With the objective of providing hostel facilities to girls students, 15 female hostels were approved under S.C.S.P./M.M. I.T. scheme. This job is being done through 100% financial cooperation of Social welfare Department and 4 hostels have been made functional.

Institutions are being identified without women’s hostel and with the objective to provide hostel facilities to girl student, 15 hostels are being constructed by the State Government. This job is being discharged by technical; education Department.
